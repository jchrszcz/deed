In this study you will be making choices between two different lotteries shown as two buttons: A and B. Your choices are divided into Blocks, and you will be making choices for 3 blocks.

Each block will ask you to make choices of two types: choices from Description and from Experience. The choices may appear in any order (e.g., Description first and Experience second, or vice versa).

In making choices from Experience, you will be allowed to explore the outcomes of the two buttons for a fixed number of times, before you make one consequential choice that will determine your bonus payment.

In making choices from Description, you will be shown the actual possible outcomes and their associated probabilities before you make a choice that will determine your bonus payment.

You will be shown the total number of points obtained and the corresponding dollar value at the end of each block.strong> Your goal is to complete the study with as many points as possible.

The cumulative points obtained from all the decisions you make across all the Blocks will be converted into your bonus payment at a rate of $xxx for xxx points. This bonus payment will be added to your base payment of $2.50.

Good luck!
